# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers, architects, contractors, and procurement stakeholders across Europe, Africa, and the MENA region who need a dependable partner for sourcing premium construction materials from China.

## Product Purpose

CoreBuild presents one accountable partner for sourcing, quality control, consolidation, and logistics across Guangzhou's manufacturing network. The site should build confidence and turn qualified project interest into direct contact.

## Brand Personality

Direct, calm, and expert. Industrial precision meets architectural luxury, with authority expressed through facts rather than promotional language.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic construction templates, bright product-on-white catalog photography, exaggerated marketing language, crowded layouts, excessive accent colors, and app-like rounded cards.

## Design Principles

- Make accountability and operational control immediately clear.
- Let architectural imagery establish scale and material quality.
- Communicate with quiet authority and specific language.
- Use restrained visual emphasis so each branded moment has weight.
- Keep the path from project interest to contact obvious.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, preserve visible keyboard focus, use descriptive labels and alternative text, and respect reduced-motion preferences throughout the experience.
